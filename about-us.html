About Evodesigns and Construction

Excellence in Construction Since 1998

Our Story

At EvoDesign, we bring over three decades of experience in engineering, design, and construction to every project we undertake. Founded by Mr. Benny Joseph, a seasoned professional with a distinguished 30-year career as a Gazetted Assistant Engineer (Retired), our firm is rooted in a legacy of precision, quality, and community service.

Specializing in residential and commercial construction, we pride ourselves on delivering tailored solutions that seamlessly blend functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability. With a deep understanding of local needs and a commitment to excellence, we aim to create spaces that not only meet but exceed expectations.

Core Values

  • Precision: Attention to detail in every project, ensuring accuracy and perfection.
  • Quality: Delivering superior craftsmanship that stands the test of time.
  • Innovation: Incorporating cutting-edge techniques and ideas to provide creative solutions.
  • Sustainability: Embracing eco-friendly practices to create sustainable and responsible designs.
